The deal

What changes for you

  • Cleaner sessions, without thinking about it.
  • Lighter context, fewer tokens burned.
  • A streak that signals discipline at a glance.

What we'll never do

  • Reward volume over cleanliness.
  • Crown only veterans — newcomers have their own map.
  • Punish absence. Your pet hibernates, never dies.

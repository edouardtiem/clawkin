the pact

what we'll never do to your pet.

01

Reward volume.

Daily pellet cap. 20 clean tasks beat 250 noisy ones. Quality multiplier in every score.

02

Crown only the veterans.

Local leaderboards by city, region, country. A newcomer can top their city in one month, even if they're #50,000 worldwide.

03

Punish absence.

Your pet never dies. It hibernates when you're away and wakes up happy when you return. Streaks pause, they don't reset.

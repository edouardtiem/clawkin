clawkin — the pet that lives in your Claude Code terminal.

A 12×12 pixel creature fed by Claude Code lifecycle hooks. Grows with clean sessions, hibernates when you're away. No game. No notifications. No daemon.

live statusline — what your followers will see.

How it works
  • Lives in your Claude Code statusline and shell prompt.
  • Fed automatically by lifecycle hooks — no command to run.
  • Level 1 to 10 000. One unique creature per level, shared worldwide.
  • Local leaderboards by city, region, country — a newcomer can still top their map.
  • Two text files edited. Zero admin, zero daemon. Works behind corporate IT.
The pact
  • We don't reward volume.
  • A newcomer can top their city in one month.
  • Your pet never dies. Absence is not punished.
Install
curl -sL clawkin.sh | sh

The installer edits ~/.zshrc and ~/.claude/settings.json. That's it. Uninstall = remove two lines.
